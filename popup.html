Help Guide

How to play

Welcome to Cookie Clicker!

The goal is simple: Bake as many cookies as you can! Here is everything you need to know to become the ultimate baker.

The Basics

Click the big cookie to earn cookies. You can also press ENTER on your keyboard! Use these cookies to buy items in the store. The more items you have, the faster you get cookies.

Buildings

Buildings bake cookies for you automatically, even when you aren't clicking!

  • Cursor: Clicks for you (Low efficiency).
  • Grandma: A nice grandma to bake cookies.
  • Farm: Grows cookies from cookie seeds.
  • Mine: Mines cookie dough from the earth.
  • Factory: Mass produces cookies.

Upgrades

Click the "Upgrades" tab to buy special Mice. These increase your Cookies Per Click. Buying a Plastic Mouse or Iron Mouse makes your manual clicks much stronger!

Prestige

Once you reach 1 Million Cookies, you can Prestige. This resets your game (you lose all buildings and cookies), but you gain a permanent Multiplier. This makes your next run much faster!

Saving

The game saves automatically every second, and immediately whenever you buy something or click the cookie.
